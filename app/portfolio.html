Bloc Jams

Bloc-Jams is a music playing site, with complete integration of DOM scripting. This is my first site I have made with this level of complexity. It was a challenging project, but well worth it in the end. Many aspects of the site needed to be debugged and modified for perfect performance. I first completed the site in Javascript, then refactored to Jquery, and then switched it completley over to AngularJS. I learned a ton along the way, and cant wait to keep building and keep growing. Jam away!
Problems to tackle in building Bloc-Jams:
  • When a song is played, that is the only song that can play.
  • When a song is paused/played the different icons change to pause or play buttons.
  • Integrating the player bar to play/pause and to use the next and previous buttons.
  • Muting the songs.
  • Have the player bar update the time as the song plays.
  • Being able to move the player bar to a different part of the song.
  • Being able to change the volume of the song.
    • I learned a lot about the logic of coding and figured out logically how to get from point A to point B.

Camp Chatter

Camp Chatter is a chat room build with AngularJS for all the future campers at the incredible awesome camp of Camp Chatter. There are chat rooms for each type of activity/group, which gives campers the ability to socialize with other like-minded campers. Everyone can see the user that has written the message. The app can also be used after camp to keep all campers and staff in touch. Skills Used: AngularJS Check out the site: Here

Bloccit

Bloccit is a Ruby on Rails application for creating posts. The app allows for users to create posts, and each post is nested under a topic. When signed in, users may comment on posts and mark them as a favorite. When a post is marked as a favorite, and email confirmation is sent to the owner of the post that their post was marked as a favorite. Skills Used: Ruby on Rails Checkout out the site: Here

Blocipedia

Blocipedia is an application where one can create wikis. If one is a preimum user, they can add collaborators to your wikis, and mark wikis as private. Skills used: Ruby on Rails - Devise gem for authorization The site can be viewed Here
