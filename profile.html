Profile

As someone with a passion for creating, I find Web Development and Design two things that excite me. I find great satisfaction in going from a blank text editor to a fully working site. With my background in both the technical and the visual, I am a perfect candidate to use all my skills to bring a site to life.

My skills include: Photoshop, Illustrator, and Indesign, HTML, CSS, Wordpress, Javascript, Jquery, Angular, and Ruby on Rails. I currently work as a freelancer doing graphic and web design projects, and studying Web Development at Bloc - an online coding bootcamp.

My previous experience includes doing PR for Kedma Israel and running their Medical Clown Internship program, working as an a UX Designer Intern for Popinz, a startup company connecting parents and babysitters, and interning at Video Activism. Please view my portfolio at www.bilmesdesign.net.
